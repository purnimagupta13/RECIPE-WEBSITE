Pizza

Ingredients

  • Whole flour
  • Yeast
  • Vegetables
  • Cheese
  • Pizza sauce
  • Oil

Steps

  1. Ferment the yeast
  2. Knead the dough
  3. Flatten the dough
  4. Add pizza sauce
  5. Add the toppings of choice
  6. Add generous amount of cheese
  7. Cook in the oven for 10 mins
